Our Product

Campfirestix was created to solve an age old problem. You're sitting by the fire in great company. Aunt Sue brings out a pack of your favourite weiners to cook over the fire as our ancestors did. You've almost achieved the perfect cook when *snap*, your weiner is ravished in flames.

Why not use a skewer? The poorly designed skewers we are familiar with, often result in the same fate! The poor design and material quality often results in a cheap, flimsy skewer difficult to control to achieve that idillic golden-brown. Using low-grade stainless steel allows the metal to bend (especially when heated), which is where gravity takes over. Aunt Sue! I dropped another one...

Our Story

Campfirestix began on the shores of Georgian Bay. Situated by the lake, the Swiderski family created many Hallmark memories at their cottage. One of these memories involves camping on the beach roasting marshmallows and hot dogs. After consistently running into issues finding the ideal stick, or a functional skewer, Old Man Swiderski took the challenge to his work bench.

Essentially being born with a Campfirestix in his hand, Ryan Swiderski felt all campers could benefit from a more refined design. His pursuit naturally began on the shores of Georgian Bay, as they had when he was a child.

The handle of each Campfirestix is sustainably collected driftwood that has naturally washed ashore the beautiful Georgian Bay. No two Campfirestix are the same!
